Setup in 60 Seconds

1

Enable ADB Debugging

On your Android TV: Settings → Developer Options → USB Debugging → ON

2

Enable Network ADB

Same menu: ADB over network → ON. Note the IP address shown.

3

Connect from AirHQ

Open AirHQ, enter the IP, tap connect. Approve on your TV. Done.
