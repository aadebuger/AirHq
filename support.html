⚡ Quick Setup Guide

  1. On your Android TV, go to Settings → About and tap "Build Number" 7 times to enable Developer Options
  2. Go to Settings → Developer Options and enable USB Debugging
  3. In the same menu, enable ADB over network
  4. Note the IP address shown (or find it in Settings → Network)
  5. Open AirHQ on your iPhone, enter the IP address, and tap the WiFi icon to connect
  6. A dialog will appear on your TV — select "Always allow" and confirm

🔍 Troubleshooting

Cannot connect / Connection timeout
  • Ensure both devices are on the same WiFi network
  • Verify ADB over network is enabled
  • Check that the IP address is correct
  • Try toggling USB Debugging off and on
  • Check "AP Isolation" is disabled on your router
Connection drops when switching apps

iOS suspends background apps. AirHQ auto-reconnects when you return. If it fails, tap the WiFi icon to reconnect.

Commands timeout / "Read timeout"
  • Disconnect and reconnect
  • Device may be under heavy load — wait and retry
  • Add | head -50 to limit large output
Remote buttons not working

Verify the green indicator is shown. Some custom launchers may not respond to all key codes.

Screenshot is slow

Speed depends on your Android TV hardware. Budget boxes may take 5-15 seconds.

"Authorization Required" keeps appearing
  • Check "Always allow from this computer" on TV
  • Try Settings → Regenerate ADB Key, then reconnect

⚡ 快速设置指南

  1. 在安卓电视上，进入 设置 → 关于，连续点击"版本号"7 次开启开发者选项
  2. 进入 设置 → 开发者选项，开启 USB 调试
  3. 在同一菜单中，开启 网络 ADB 调试
  4. 记下 IP 地址（或在 设置 → 网络 中查看）
  5. 在 iPhone 上打开 AirHQ，输入 IP 地址，点击 WiFi 图标连接
  6. 电视上弹出授权对话框 — 勾选 "始终允许" 并确认

🔍 常见问题

无法连接 / 连接超时
  • 确认两台设备连接同一 WiFi
  • 确认已开启网络 ADB 调试
  • 检查 IP 地址是否正确
  • 尝试关闭再开启 USB 调试
  • 检查路由器是否开启了"AP 隔离"
切换应用后断线

iOS 会在后台挂起应用。AirHQ 返回时自动重连。失败则点击 WiFi 图标手动重连。

命令超时 / "Read timeout"
  • 断开后重新连接
  • 设备负载较高时等待后重试
  • 添加 | head -50 限制输出
遥控器按键无反应

确认绿色指示灯亮起。部分定制启动器可能不响应所有按键。

截图速度很慢

取决于安卓电视硬件。低端盒子可能需要 5-15 秒。

反复出现"需要授权"
  • 在电视端勾选"始终允许此计算机"
  • 设置中"重新生成 ADB 密钥"后重连

  • Fire TV Stick（开启 ADB）

  • 需要 Android 5.0 或更高版本
